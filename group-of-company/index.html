Group of Company


Accelution Group


Accelution Group encompasses a diverse range of entities with distinct areas of expertise. Specializing in regulatory compliance, one entity offers expert guidance in navigating complex regulations. Another operates at the intersection of the oil and gas and renewable energy industries, driving innovation. Competency and certification training services are provided by one entity, empowering professionals across various industries. Another focuses on comprehensive training in higher education and technical fields. With a focus on human capital solutions, another entity maximizes individual potential within organizations. Lastly, an investment firm actively seeks opportunities to support innovative startups and emerging businesses in the venture capital and financial market sectors. Collectively, the Accelution Group delivers excellence, expertise, and innovation across a range of industries.


Accelution Advisor Sdn Bhd


ACCELUTION ADVISORS SDN BHD (AASB) specializes in regulatory compliance services, ensuring that businesses adhere to the necessary regulations and guidelines. We provide comprehensive support and expertise to help companies navigate complex regulatory frameworks and stay in compliance with industry standards. Our core focus is on assisting businesses in meeting their regulatory obligations, mitigating risks, and maintaining a strong compliance culture. Through our services, we help organizations operate within legal boundaries, promote ethical practices, and safeguard their reputation in the market.


Accelution Energy Sdn Bhd


ACCELUTION ENERGY SDN BHD (AESB) operates at the intersection of two dynamic industries: oil and gas, and renewable energy. With a focus on sustainability and innovation, we provide comprehensive solutions and services to clients in both sectors. In the oil and gas industry, we offer expertise in exploration, production, refining, and distribution, ensuring efficient operations and compliance with industry regulations. Additionally, we actively engage in renewable energy projects, including solar, wind, and hydrogen, leveraging cutting-edge technologies to drive the transition towards cleaner and more sustainable energy sources. Our diverse portfolio and deep industry knowledge allow us to deliver tailored solutions that meet the unique challenges and opportunities presented by the oil and gas and renewable energy markets.


Hab Network Sdn Bhd


HAB NETWORK SDN BHD (HNSB) specialize in providing competency and certification training services to various industries. Our focus is on equipping individuals and organizations with the knowledge, skills, and certifications necessary to excel in their respective fields. We offer a wide range of training programs that cover essential competencies and industry-specific certifications. Whether it's in the oil and gas sector, renewable energy, engineering, healthcare, or any other industry, we have expert trainers and comprehensive training modules designed to enhance professional development and ensure compliance with industry standards. Through our rigorous training programs, we empower individuals to enhance their capabilities, advance their careers, and contribute to the success of their organizations. With a commitment to excellence and a customer-centric approach, we strive to deliver top-notch competency and certification training solutions that drive professional growth and organizational success.


Modern-Q College


MODERN-Q COLLEGE is dedicated to providing comprehensive competency and certification training in the fields of higher education and technical and vocational education and training (TVET). We offer a wide range of programs and courses designed to equip individuals with the necessary knowledge, skills, and qualifications for success in their chosen careers. Through our collaborations with esteemed institutions, we provide quality education, practical skills development, and industry exposure to empower individuals in their professional growth. Whether in higher education or TVET, we are committed to preparing individuals for the demands of the job market and supporting their career aspirations.


Mutiara Skill Synergy Sdn Bhd


MUTIARA SKILL SYNERGY SDN BHD specializes in human capital solutions, placing a strong emphasis on maximizing the potential of individuals within organizations. We offer a range of services tailored to develop, nurture, and harness the talents and capabilities of employees. From talent acquisition and recruitment strategies to leadership development and performance management, our comprehensive approach aims to optimize human resources for sustainable growth and success. By focusing on the development and well-being of individuals, we strive to create a positive and thriving work environment that fosters productivity, innovation, and long-term organizational success.


IMA Strategic Reserves Sdn Bhd


IMA STRATEGIC RESERVES SDN BHD specializes in venture capital and financial market investments, actively seeking opportunities to support innovative startups and emerging businesses. With a keen eye for market trends and potential growth, we provide strategic investment guidance and financial resources to help companies thrive and reach their full potential. Through a comprehensive approach, we aim to identify promising investment opportunities, conduct thorough due diligence, and provide ongoing support to portfolio companies. Our focus on VC and financial market investment reflects our commitment to fostering entrepreneurship, driving economic growth, and creating value for our investors and partners.
