Management Associate Programme

EMPOWERING LEADERS THAT MAKE AN IMPACT

The Sunway Management Associate Programme is a two-year programme for recent graduates with demonstrated academic excellence and leadership traits. This is how we provide proper guidance and empowering them to be entrepreneurial, nurturing well-rounded leaders ready to take the Group forward.

Learn more our Management Associate Programme today

BEGIN YOUR JOURNEY WITH US!

You will be exposed to Sunway’s business divisions, mentored by the top management, and assigned with special projects where you get to engage key stakeholders. It is a structured journey that will set you up for a successful career in years and decades to come while making an impact to the world.

Start your journey with us today

Group Internal Audit Department (GIAD) Attachment

Exposure to Sunway’s business divisions

Learn more

The GIAD Attachment offers Management Associates (MAs) the opportunity to gain exposure to different business divisions within Sunway Group, apart from your ’home’ business divisions. During the initial three months of joining Sunway, MAs will report to the GIAD team and be assigned audit projects for businesses across the Group. These projects involve auditing the internal processes and controls of the business operations, which may involve you travelling to or being based at various business locations and sites nationwide.

Personalised Career Development

For accelerated growth

Learn more

During the two-year programme, MAs will have a career development plan that clearly outlines the specific areas of work and development identified for your accelerated growth. MAs will be involved in projects and assignments which enables you to learn and gain a broader understanding and perspective of your employer’s operations and overall business direction. This provides MAs with opportunities to expand your knowledge and skills within the various business divisions.

Business Functions Attachment

Direct mentorship from top management

Learn more

Upon completing the GIAD attachment, MAs will begin your development journey with your respective business divisions, deepening your expertise in respective business verticals while developing a broad business understanding and appreciation through adjacent exposures. After the two-year programme, MAs will continue your career progression and growth within the same business division, becoming subject matter experts in your respective functions and further excelling in your chosen paths.

Holistic Leadership Development

Promote personal and professional growth

Learn more

MAs will participate in a series of learning programmes designed to promote both personal and professional growth. These programmes prioritise self-reflection, coaching, mentoring and leadership development, creating an environment that encourages continuous learning.

Special Project Assignment

Engagement with key leaders & stakeholders

Learn more

In addition to the daily responsibilities within your respective BUs, MAs will have the chance to participate in a range of projects that involve collaborating with leaders from various businesses. MAs work as a cohesive team under the guidance of industry leaders, contributing fresh and innovative ideas for various business projects. These ideas are then presented to the management team with the goal of obtaining approval for practical implementation. These special projects provide them the opportunities to engage with cross-functional teams, broaden your perspectives, and make a meaningful impact within the organisation.
