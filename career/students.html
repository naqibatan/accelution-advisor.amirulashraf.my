Scholarships

Sunway is committed to nurturing global leaders of tomorrow. We offer full scholarships covering tuition fees, living allowances and relevant incidental expenses to deserving students pursuing tertiary education in Malaysia or overseas, with career development opportunities upon graduation.

The Sunway Scholarship is open to everyone aged 25 years and below, including eligible Sunway employees and dependants.
