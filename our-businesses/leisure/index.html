Leisure

Asia’s Premier Multi-Park Destinations

Sunway Leisure owns and operates two multiple award-winning theme parks: namely Sunway Lagoon Theme Park in Sunway City Kuala Lumpur opened in 1992, and Sunway Lost World of Tambun in the historic town of Ipoh, Perak, which opened its doors to the public in November 2004.

Sunway Lagoon Theme Park, Malaysia’s premier multi-park destination located at the heart of Sunway City Kuala Lumpur, is only 30 minutes away from the city centre and Kuala Lumpur International Airport (KLIA). It offers more than 90 attractions across six parks – Water Park, Amusement Park, Wildlife Park, X Park, Scream Park, and its latest addition, Sunway Lost Lagoon.

Sunway Lost World of Tambun is Malaysia’s premier action-and-adventure family holiday destination. This self-contained wonderland is cocooned by lush tropical jungle, natural hot springs, breathtaking 260-million-year-old limestone features and ten amazing attraction parks, making it the ultimate day and night destination for a unique eco-adventure excursion for visitors of all ages.

Sunway Lost World of Tambun offers a variety of action-packed adventures, from splashing around at the waterpark, relaxing at its natural hot-springs pool to experiencing close encounters with a variety of animals at the petting zoo.

Discover Sunway Lagoon Theme Park

Sunway Lagoon Theme Park, Malaysia’s premier multi-park destination located at the heart of Sunway City Kuala Lumpur, is only 30 minutes away from the city centre and Kuala Lumpur International Airport (KLIA). It offers more than 90 attractions across six parks – Water Park, Amusement Park, Wildlife Park, X Park, Scream Park and its latest addition, Sunway Lost Lagoon.

Named “Asia’s Best Attraction” for four consecutive years by the International Association of Amusement Parks and Attractions (“IAAPA”), the 88-acre theme park excites visitors of all ages with wholesome family-friendly fun to adrenaline-pumping thrills.

The park is also home to attractions such as the World’s Largest Water Ride – Vuvuzela, World’s First Waterplexx 5D, World’s Largest Man-Made Sandy Surf Beach, Malaysia’s First Interactive Zoo and FlowRider, Malaysia’s First Surf Simulator. Also among the attractions are the Captain’s Splash Adventure, Primeval, Jungle Fury, Monsoon 360, and many more.

Being one of the region’s most sought-after concert and event venues, Sunway Lagoon has hosted some of the biggest international lifestyle, sporting and music events including the latest K-Pop acts.

Discover Sunway Lost World of Tambun

Sunway Lost World of Tambun is an action-packed, wholesome family adventure destination. This self-contained wonderland is cocooned within a lush tropical jungle consisting of natural hot springs and breathtaking 260-million-year-old limestone features. Painstakingly etched into this natural environment are ten amazing attraction parks, coined by locals and foreigners alike to be the ultimate day and night destination for a unique family-friendly eco-adventure excursion.

Just a stone’s throw away is the Lost World Hotel, Glamping and Malayana Floating Villa, a perfect respite and snooze chamber after a long day of adventure. True to its name, Sunway Lost World of Tambun offers not only a ‘lost paradise’ that promises fun and wholesome experience for all ages, but also a conducive learning environment with a range of fun-educational elements around the park.

It is the only theme park in Southeast Asia featuring natural hot springs coupled with an array of contemporary attractions and rides. Business entities and MICE groups will also find this resort a haven for team-building activities, given its facilities which include seven specially curated function rooms with a capacity ranging from 10 to 200 people.

A hidden gem within Malaysia, Sunway Lost World of Tambun successfully converts what was previously a tin-mining wasteland into an iconic landmark, without encroaching on its natural surroundings. – Historic, educational and entertaining at the same time, it is a must-visit site unlike any other within the Southeast Asian region. This, among many others, truly befits Sunway Lost World of Tambun’s mantra of being “More Than Just A Theme Park!”.
