A Master Community Developer

As a Master Community Developer, Sunway Property has established a reputation in developing innovative residential, commercial and retail properties. Our aspiration to build sustainable townships began with the rehabilitation of Sunway City Kuala Lumpur from a tin-mining wasteland to a wonderland. From there, we have proceeded to replicate and remodel cities – fulfilling the United Nations Sustainable Development Goals to create smart sustainable cities and communities.

We aspire to build a better tomorrow by creating integrated spaces where communities live, learn, work, play in a safe, healthy and connected environment. Our unique Build-Own-Operate (BOO) business model powers our ability as a Master Community Developer to create a unique win-win proposition through sustainable communities where future generations can continue to thrive.

A Community Builder, Entrepreneurship, and Enriching Lives are the values that continue to drive our community-centric mission to deliver unrivalled products in the industry that not only serve the needs of today’s generation, but for generations to come. Today, we have delivered an extensive portfolio of properties in growth markets such as Malaysia, China, Singapore, Cambodia and Vietnam.

As our communities’ growth partner, our steadfast commitment to their long-term success is evidenced in our master-planned communities of Sunway City Kuala Lumpur, Sunway City Ipoh, Sunway Valleycity, Sunway Damansara, Sunway Velocity and Sunway City Iskandar Puteri. For more than a decade, Sunway Property has been ranked as Malaysia’s top developer at The Edge Top 10 Property Developers Awards.

Sunway City Kuala Lumpur
Learn more

Sprawling 800 acres, Sunway City Kuala Lumpur is Malaysia’s first fully-integrated green township as certified by Green Building Index (GBI), and a Low Carbon City as certified by the Malaysian Institute of Planners. Sunway City Kuala Lumpur was built on a tin-mining wasteland and is a product of outstanding rehabilitation and restoration efforts. Located just 20 minutes from the Kuala Lumpur city centre, Sunway City Kuala Lumpur has a built-up area of more than 60 million square feet comprising world-class facilities, and attracts some 42 million visits every year.

It encompasses an enviable portfolio of more than 11,000 residential and commercial units, in addition to the multi award-winning hospitality, retail, leisure, healthcare and education components. Our city also aims to be self-sustainable through our assets such as maintaining urban forestry, rehabilitating our two lakes located in Sunway South Quay and Sunway Lagoon via our water treatment plant, which allows the water collected to be used for our commercial buildings, launching our Sunway Seeding Inspiration and Leadership via Knowledge (SILK) corporate initiative to teach students to create their own urban farms, as well as a plethora of other initiatives to be a smart sustainable city.

Sunway City Kuala Lumpur is also regarded as a safe city, and aims to become a model smart sustainable city, nurturing the communities that live, learn, work, and play within it by creating an ecosystem that will inspire a healthy, safe and interconnected society for generations to come. Today, Sunway City Kuala Lumpur remains one of Southeast Asia’s premier tourist destinations.

The Selangor Town and Country Planning Development recently recognised Sunway City Kuala Lumpur as an Inclusive Development. In addition to that, in 2017, Sunway City Kuala Lumpur was recognised as Top Smart City Initiative in Asia Pacific excluding Japan, under the Smart Buildings category by IDC Government Insights’ IDC Smart City Asia Pacific Awards (SCAPA). It also won best township with “The Five Elements Development” Award at the StarProperty.my Awards 2016.

Sunway City Ipoh
Learn more

Sunway City Ipoh (SCI) is the largest integrated township in Malaysia’s state of Perak, spanning approximately 1,350 acres with a GDV of about RM2 billion. It was conceived in 1996 and is driven by Sunway as the catalyst of growth for the Eastern Development Corridor of Ipoh.

One of the key elements of SCI’s master plan was to work around the area’s natural terrains. SCI is strategically located in the midst of tropical greenery and majestic 260-million-year-old limestone hills. It has also retained 120,000 trees in the township. Thus, we have taken steps to preserve the natural environment, such as the hills, caves and lakes, which also serve as water retention ponds, in our bid to develop sustainable townships aligned with the UN-SDG.

SCI is also easily reachable via direct flights to Ipoh from Singapore and Johor Bahru, and more in the future. Frequent Electric Train Service (ETS) has helped public mobility between Kuala Lumpur, Ipoh and Padang Besar.

SCI also houses the award-winning theme park Sunway Lost World of Tambun and the luxurious Banjaran Hotsprings Retreat. The township receives an annual average of 1.5 million visits from all over the world. It is also a winner of the FIABCI Malaysia Property Award for the Best Master Plan Township in 2010.

Sunway City Iskandar Puteri
Learn more

Sunway City Iskandar Puteri, an 1,800-acre world-class township with a RM30 billion GDV, is envisioned to be the quintessence of good living in Iskandar Malaysia. Fronting the Straits of Johor, the township is unmatched in natural beauty – surrounded by a riverine estate, a pristine mangrove forest, as well as traversed by the majestic 7km Pendas River, all of which are preserved and kept in its natural state during development – in line with our goal in cultivating a sustainable township.

The city within the Iskandar Puteri Malaysia megalopolis will integrate Sunway’s seven synergistic key components of education, hospitality, theme park, retail, residential, commercial and healthcare – all within one secure, convenient, master-planned development.

Partnering with Khazanah Nasional Berhad and Iskandar Investment Berhad (IIB), Sunway City Iskandar Puteri is poised to be the proxy for Iskandar Puteri Malaysia. Sunway is also one of the largest landowners in Iskandar Puteri, Malaysia.

Sunway Valleycity
Learn more

Sunway Valleycity (SVC) is a comprehensive and all-encompassing development with mixed components including a shopping mall, a medical centre, residential suites, retail shops, office towers, and an education hub. In our bid for a sustainable development, the shopping mall, retail shops, office towers and residential suites have also been officially certified by Green Building Index (GBI) and GreenRE.

It is located within a mature neighbourhood, and in close proximity to amenities such as public and private schools and colleges, hospital, hotels, shopping malls, eateries and services. It is also easily accessible via Jalan Paya Terubong which will eventually lead to Air Itam, and is well connected to the Penang Bridge and Penang International Airport.

Sunway Velocity
Learn more

Sunway Velocity is a 23-acre self-sustainable integrated development located in Kuala Lumpur. It comprises residential, commercial, mall, healthcare, education, hotel and retail components.

At its epicenter is V Boulevard, a vehicle-free deck lined with alfresco dining hot spots and retail shops that connects all components of Sunway Velocity. Additionally, there are two link bridges installed with CCTVs and stationed with auxiliary police that connect the development to two MRT and one LRT stations.

Within lies a two-acre park abundant in lush greenery, water features, a playground and a jogging track. The park aims to promote a healthy lifestyle for all within Sunway Velocity.

Following the success of Sunway Velocity, Sunway Velocity TWO elevates city living to a whole new level of vibrancy. This development comprises three phases built on 8.5 acres and is directly connected to the comprehensive amenities in Sunway Velocity via elevated covered walkways. Sunway Velocity TWO has also incorporated sustainability into its development. Sunway Velocity TWO Tower has been certified Gold, whereas Sunway Velocity TWO Corporate Centre has been certified Platinum by Green Building Index and GreenRE.
