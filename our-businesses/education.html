Education

An Education Hub of Excellence in Asia

Sunway Education Group is committed to providing quality education for all, regardless of race, creed or financial standing since its establishment in 1987. As a pioneer of private education in Malaysia, we have helped transform the landscape of education in the nation.

Our educational institutions and entities are owned and governed by the Jeffrey Cheah Foundation (JCF), Malaysia’s largest education-focused social enterprise.  Through our unique not-for-profit structure, operating surpluses are channelled back into the institutions or disbursed as scholarships, research grants and expansion funds for faculty and facilities to ensure quality education in perpetuity.

Through the JCF, we have established partnerships with world-renowned education institutions.  These collaborations lend international qualifications to our educational programmes, elevating the teaching and research standards of our universities and in Malaysia.

This is in line with our aim to continuously transform ourselves and our educational institutions into a world-class brand, providing accessible quality education to all.  To date, we encompass 23 educational entities including Sunway University, Sunway College, Sunway International School and more.

Sunway International School
Learn more

Sunway International School is the only international school in Malaysia to offer the Ontario (Canada) Secondary School curriculum, which is ranked among the world’s best in school education. According to the Organisation for Economic Co-operation and Development (OECD), the Ontario (Canada) education system has consistently ranked among the top five performing countries in the world.

In addition, the International Baccalaureate (IB) programme offers a unique academic education which emphasises students’ personal development. We aim to do more than curricula by developing inquiring, knowledgeable and caring young people who are motivated to succeed.

Our teachers are certified by professional teaching bodies.  Their professional qualifications and classroom experiences mean that our teachers are well versed in implementing a wide variety of teaching and learning strategies, along with different types of assessment and evaluation methods to ensure all students experience exceptional learning opportunities.

We are equipped with state-of-the-art learning facilities. Our e-learning course management system permits teachers to share daily assignments and learning resources with students and parents.

Jeffrey Sachs Center on Sustainable Development
Learn more

The Jeffrey Sachs Center on Sustainable Development is a regional centre of excellence that advances the achievement of the 17 Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs) in Malaysia and Southeast Asia, tackling the sustainability agenda through education, training and research and policy advisory. We focus on research and policy practice, delivering world-class programmes to train students, practitioners and policy leaders on sustainable initiatives while working in collaboration with industry, government bodies, NGOs and universities worldwide to develop solutions related to the SDGs.

Launched in December 2016, we operate in Sunway University under the supervision of Professor Jeffrey D. Sachs, one of the world’s leading experts in sustainable development. We were established out of a $10 million gift from the Jeffrey Cheah Foundation (JCF) to the UN Sustainable Development Solutions Network (SDSN).

Southeast Asia's Regional Hub

We serve as the regional hub of the Sustainable Development Solutions Network

Jeffrey Cheah Institute on Southeast Asia
Learn more

Established on 18 March 2014, the Jeffrey Cheah Institute on Southeast Asia (JCI) is an independent public policy think-tank, based at Sunway University. Our mission is to develop efficient and equitable solutions to some of the region’s most pressing development problems.

Our research programmes are grouped around three core areas: economic development, governance, and social progress. We work closely with the Jeffrey Sachs Center on Sustainable Development for the advancement of the 17 Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs) adopted by the United Nations in 2016.

International Partnerships

Our partnerships include Harvard University, Columbia University, ISEAS – Yusof Ishak Institute, Keio University, Korea Institute for International Economic Policy (KIEP) and Shanghai Jiao Tong University

Training for the Future

We intend to raise the next generation of leaders with theoretical knowledge, technical knowledge and implementation abilities through our postgraduate programmes

Bridging World-Class Institutions

We administer the Jeffrey Cheah  Travel Grants to allow scholars and researchers from Sunway and Harvard to travel between both institutions for research, knowledge-sharing and cross-cultural communication
