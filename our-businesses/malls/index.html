Malls

Among Malaysia’s Largest Mall Owner-Operators

The mall division owns and manages a portfolio of leading retail malls across Malaysia, strategically located in master-planned townships or mixed developments. We manage eight physical and digital retail malls with a combined net lettable area (NLA), of existing projects and those in the pipeline, to an excess of 7.7 million sq. ft.

They include our flagship retail property Sunway Pyramid in Sunway City Kuala Lumpur; Sunway Velocity Mall, Kuala Lumpur; Sunway Big Box Retail Park, Sunway City Iskandar Puteri, Johor; Sunway Citrine, Sunway City Iskandar Puteri, Johor; Sunway Putra Mall, Kuala Lumpur; Sunway Giza, Sunway Damansara; and Sunway Carnival Mall, Penang

Sunway Malls is on track to become Malaysia’s largest shopping-mall owner and operator with five more assets in the pipeline, and targets to increase its NLA to 10.2 million square feet in the near future.

Discover Sunway Pyramid

Opened in 1997, Sunway Pyramid is Malaysia’s first themed shopping and entertainment mall, boasting 2 million sq. ft. of net lettable area (NLA). With an Egyptian-inspired pyramid and a monumental lion standing guard at its entrance, our mall features five precincts – Fashion Central, Oasis Boulevard, Asian Avenue, Marrakesh, and The Link, offering a variety of specialty outlets.

Easily accessible via an air-conditioned walkway on the First Floor, Sunway Pyramid West boasts an area of 100,000 sq. ft., with a plethora of family-friendly F&B and retail offerings as well as an all-in-one child enrichment and edutainment centre.

Discover Sunway Giza Mall

Opened since 2009, Sunway Giza Mall is a 100,000 sq. ft., three-storey mall with a covered boulevard. Our 6.4-acre specialty retail centre comprises a vivacious mix of 48 units of shop offices and a three-storey lifestyle neighbourhood mall with the convenience of an 850-bay basement carpark.

As Kota Damansara’s friendly neighbourhood mall, our covered boulevard that links the shop offices and mall allows all-weather al-fresco activities to take place throughout the year. This unique design also paves the way for the implementation of a 2-in-1 experience – daytime shopping within the mall and night entertainment at the covered boulevard area. The mall also incorporated energy efficient T5 lighting with colourful illumination, adding to its design and architecture.

Environmentally Conscious Design

The first in Malaysia to utilise 13 big-S fans along its covered boulevard walkway, with exhaust ducts to help the air circulation. Most importantly, each of these 2 horsepower fans is powered only by 7 amp, a truly energy saving design that also reduces the boulevard temperature 4 degrees lower than the outdoor temperature

Aesthetically Efficient Lighting

We hold the distinction as the first boulevard to be lit with colourful, energy-saving LEDs

Discover Sunway Carnival Mall

Opened in 2007, Sunway Carnival Mall is a stylist 4-storey shopping mall strategically located within the town centre of Seberang Jaya. The mall recently expanded in 2022, sporting a total of 1.0 million net lettable area (NLA), 350 shops and 3,200 parking bays.

The new, bold neo-futuristic façade of Sunway Carnival Mall has transformed the skyline of mainland Penang with a distinctive iconic feature – tilting and reclining ‘Cubes’ that signify dynamism and progressiveness of a modern city. The mall is currently undergoing a refurbishment exercise, and is expected to be complete by Q4 2024.

Discover Sunway Big Box Retail Park

Sited on a 24-acre plot of land, Sunway Big Box Retail Park is a catalyst for a new retail and leisure experience situated at Sunway City Iskandar Puteri. With an X-treme Park and a hotel adjacent to the mall, we are set to become a key attraction in the Southern region of Malaysia.
