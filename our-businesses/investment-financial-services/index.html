Investment & Financial Services

Investing In A Better Tomorrow

Sunway Investment & Financial Services looks into high-value investment possibilities, offering financial services as well as business solutions. These investments are done through our current investment arm – Sunway Ventures to build on services that further expands Sunway’s exercise to diversify its business portfolio.

Sunway Ventures’ diverse portfolio encompasses Sunway Pals, a loyalty programme; Sunway Money, an e-remittance service; Sunway SCF, an early payment programme for companies to gain capital; Sunway iLabs, a hub to foster entrepreneurship and innovations; Sunway PopBox, an automated parcel locker for online shoppers; and lastly, Sunway Pharmacy, a pharmacy retail chain under Sunway Group.

Sunway Pals
Learn more

Sunway Pals, Malaysia’s fastest-growing and Sunway Group’s flagship integrated loyalty programme, was launched in February 2014 and currently has more than 900,000 members. Our customers and members receive reward points and a range of privileges when they make property purchases or spend at participating Sunway-owned malls, hotels, theme parks, colleges, universities, hospitals and its array of participating merchants. Members also enjoy the benefits of cashless parking with no surcharge.

Our data-driven customer engagement allows us to strive for better customer loyalty and retention. In addition, our partnership with airline programmes such as Enrich and financial institutions such as HSBC, UOB and Maybank has allowed us to provide travelling and other boosted benefits through our point exchange programme.

Sunway Money
Learn more

Sunway Money is Malaysia’s first completely online and licensed remittance house wholly owned by a Malaysian public-listed company. We provide convenience for overseas money transfers at a competitive pricing point. Our services are fully licensed by Bank Negara Malaysia.

By utilising innovative fintech solutions and partnering with FPX (an online real-time payment gateway operated by PayNet Group), we are able to source competitive real-time exchange rates, minimise our operating costs and offer our services around the clock.

Sunway iLabs
Learn more

Launched in 2017, Sunway iLabs aims to foster entrepreneurship, stimulate market-driven innovations and help entrepreneurs become more competitive in this rapidly changing environment.

We are a unique, non-profit, smart partnership between Sunway Group, Sunway Ventures/SunSea Capital and Sunway University.

As Malaysia Digital Hub and Premier Digital Tech University, we are well connected with the wider startup ecosystem in Malaysia and abroad through collaborations and strategic partnerships, enabling entrepreneurs to scale their businesses faster.

We were also recently awarded Outstanding Property Development for Startups 2019 by the Malaysian Venture Capital and Private Equity Association (MVCA) for having established the most complete innovation ecosystem. We operate The Makerspace in Sunway University and iLabs, a co-working and innovation space.

PopBox is an automated parcel locker that provides contactless delivery for online shoppers, allowing users to send and receive their parcels in a convenient and secure way.

Sunway Pharmacy
Learn more

In 2016, Sunway Pharmacy opened Malaysia’s very first pharmacy store in a petrol station at Caltex. Fast forwarding two years to 2018, we have increased to a total of 10 outlets in the Klang Valley region and is still rapidly expanding today.

We are a pharmacy retail chain under Sunway with experienced registered pharmacists stationed in every outlet to provide customers with the best medical advice as nothing is more important than their well-being.

To further ensure the delivery of the best care possible, every pharmacy is fully stocked with a wide set of medicines and equipped with ample space for counselling sessions.

Our pharmacies presently offer personal care, feminine care, baby care, skin care, exclusive beauty care products, vitamins and supplements, medical equipment, mobility aids and many more. And we are constantly adding newer and innovative products into the inventory as we progress further ahead.
