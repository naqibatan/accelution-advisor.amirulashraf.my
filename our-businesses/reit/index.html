Reit & Other Real Estate Funds

One of the Largest Diversified REITs in Malaysia

Sunway REIT is one of Malaysia’s largest diversified Real Estate Investment Trusts (REIT) with a diverse portfolio, strategically located across award-winning integrated townships in key locations within Klang Valley, Penang and Perak. Our portfolio comprises 20 properties which include retail malls, hotels, offices, industrial properties, a medical centre and a purpose-built education campus. The asset portfolio has a total property value of RM9.1 billion with a total gross floor area (GFA) of approximately 19.2 million square feet as of 30 June 2023.

Our objective is to deliver sustainable distributions and growth in total return to unitholders over the long term via active asset management, yield-accretive acquisitions supported by optimal capital and risk management. With a conscious strategy aligned with the United Nations Sustainable Development Goals, our aim is to prioritise the development of sustainability programmes that are self-sustaining for positive economic, environmental and societal impact.

Sunway RE Capital’s objective is to invest in real estate for recurring income along with a fund management platform. Its key investment focus is commercial properties and purpose-built student accommodation (PBSA) primarily in the United Kingdom.

Sunway RE Capital is one of the sponsors of Elite Commercial REIT (ECR) which is listed in Singapore since February 2020 with a market capitalisation of £147mil as at 30 June 2023. ECR is the first and only UK-Focused S-REIT with a portfolio of 155 properties with an AUM of £466.2mil as at 31 December 2022.

Sunway RE Capital’s investment in PBSA is via a private fund established in Singapore, Sunway Residence Trust II, with an AUM of £75 mil, comprising a portfolio of four properties with 828 beds in key cities in the UK and all in Russell Group cities (i.e. Bristol, Manchester, Southampton and Sheffield).
