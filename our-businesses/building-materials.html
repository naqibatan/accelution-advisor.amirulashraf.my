Building Materials

Among Malaysia’s Leading and Largest Manufacturers

Established in 2010, the Sunway Building Materials Group (BMG) comprises three core segments – Sunway Paving Solution for concrete pavement, Sunway VCP for vitrified clay pipes and Sunway Spun Piles. Our eight manufacturing facilities are ISO accredited in Quality Management System (ISO 9001:2015) and Environment Management System (IS)14001:2015).

We continue to produce high-quality products in compliance with local and international standards. We focus on bettering our products and processes through innovation to be more effective and efficient in our business operations, as well as providing green innovation products and services for an eco-friendly environment, in alignment with the United Nations Sustainable Development Goals. We were also named Malaysia’s Best Employers by global talent consultancy Aon Hewitt in 2016 and 2018.

Sunway Paving Solutions manufactures and supplies Interlocking Concrete Pavers (ICP) and Compressed Concrete Paving Slabs (Eurotiles). We produce pavers through five plants across Malaysia, which are strategically located at Batang Kali – two plants (Selangor), Senai (Johor), Nibong Tebal (Pulau Pinang) and Marang (Terengganu).

Aligned with our commitment to the United Nations Sustainable Development Goals, we continue to innovate and manufacture products which are in compliance with Eco-Label and Green Label requirements.

Sunway Spun Pile
Learn more

In 2015, Sunway built its first Malaysian spun pile plant at Batang Kali, Selangor, bringing together its regional experience around Asia to benefit its clients locally and globally.

The Batang Kali plant uses state-of-the-art machinery and technology from Japan, a front runner in the spun pile industry. In addition, Sunway Spun Pile (Zhuhai) Co., Ltd. specialises in the production, sales and after-sales service of large PHC piles (Prestressed High Strength Concrete piles), with a diameter of 700mm and above.

We were established as a subsidiary of Sunway in Malaysia in 2008. Sunway Spun Pile Zhuhai owns the largest and most advanced production line for spun piles in Zhuhai, China with an annual capacity of 550,000m across two plants.

We manufacture super large spun piles by adopting high pressure steaming technology, allowing product/concrete strength of more than C80, which enhances resistance during impact.
