Hospitality

Asia’s Leading International Hotel Group

Sunway Hotels & Resorts is one of Asia’s leading international hotel groups and developers of premium hotels. We have 12 hotels and resorts across Malaysia, Cambodia and Vietnam, with close to 4,000 guest rooms, suites and villas in total.

We continue to raise the bar for the most authentic Asian travel experiences by delivering exceptional services and care at our hotels and resorts. With a focus on brand and development standards, we consistently raise the benchmark while maintaining our authenticity.

The division’s diverse portfolio of hospitality assets and services includes ownership and management of a cluster of hotels with large-scale convention, meeting and exhibition facilities in an integrated city, on top of private villa destinations, a wellness retreat and a collection of three, four, and five-star hotels in mixed-use developments.

Sunway Hotels & Resorts is committed to the United Nations Sustainable Development Goals (UN-SDG). We continue to align our business model, operating strategies and corporate culture with sustainable practices and solutions in line with the UN-SDG agenda. We have woven sustainable development into the very fabric of our existing hotels in Malaysia, Cambodia and Vietnam, as well as those planned for the future.
