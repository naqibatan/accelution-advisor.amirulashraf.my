Healthcare

Malaysia’s Leading Healthcare Provider

Sunway Healthcare Group (SHG) is a leading integrated private healthcare group in Malaysia. Its flagship quaternary hospital Sunway Medical Centre, Sunway City Kuala Lumpur (SCKL), was established in 1999. Since then, we have expanded its network to include tertiary hospitals Sunway Medical Centre Velocity, Cheras; and Sunway Medical Centre Penang. The three hospitals have a combined capacity of some 1150 beds and more than 400 specialist consultants.

We also operate ancillary healthcare businesses: Sunway Specialist Centre Damansara, Sunway Medical Centre Singapore, Sunway Traditional and Complementary Medicine (TCM) Centre, Sunway Home Healthcare and Sunway Fertility Centre (Kuching). Additionally, we operate Sunway Sanctuary, which comprises 235 suites for senior living in Phase One, that sit right above Sunway Medical Centre at SCKL. As part of our expansion strategy to provide more communities with access to quality healthcare, SHG will increase our footprint all over Malaysia with tertiary hospitals in Damansara (Selangor), Iskandar Puteri (Johor), Ipoh (Perak), Kota Bharu (Kelantan), and a rehabilitation hospital in Paya Terubong (Penang). With this growth, we will have a combined capacity of over 3,000 beds in its network of hospitals by 2030.

We proactively support the nation’s agenda in raising healthcare standards in Malaysia by reaching out and educating the community on health issues and the importance of healthcare, in line with the vision of the Ministry of Health as well as the United Nations Sustainable Development Goals to achieve better health for all.

With the aim to shape the future of healthcare, we seek to enhance collaboration with renowned universities and hospitals in the domain of medicine and medical education. These include University of Cambridge, University of Oxford, Sunway University, Jeffrey Cheah School of Medicine and Health Sciences, Royal Papworth Hospital, Royal College of Physicians, Harvard Medical School and The Royal College of Pathologists Australasia. The partnership with University of Cambridge, in particular, also includes recognising SHG’s Clinical Research Centre as the sole Regional Site Partner to conduct and coordinate clinical trials. With some of the nation’s most skilled and experienced healthcare professionals and state-of-the-art equipment among its network of hospitals, SHG aims to set new benchmarks for quality medical care in the region, serving thousands of international patients from over 170 countries.

Discover Sunway Medical Centre @ Sunway City Kuala Lumpur

Established in November 1999, Sunway Medical Centre is an Australian Council on Healthcare Standards (ACHS) accredited private hospital located in Malaysia’s first fully integrated green township of Sunway City Kuala Lumpur.

We offer comprehensive quaternary healthcare services, with 28 centres of excellence, and over 60 medical specialties, coupled with a robust support system comprising over 3,300 healthcare professionals. The cutting-edge technology available in Sunway Medical Centre includes, the Varian TrueBeam STx, da Vinci Robotics Surgical System, Elekta Leksell Gamma Knife Icon, Zeiss Intrabeam IORT, Siemens xSPECT-CT, Siemens Somatom Force Dual Source CT scanner and Radixact-X9 Tomotherapy. We also implement multiple sustainability initiatives in line with the UN-SDG such as heightening public awareness of the importance of healthcare through free health screenings, public forums and medical symposia, as well as our Nursing Scholarship programme.

Given the growing medical tourism industry, we are embarking on an extensive expansion plan next to the existing hospital building to better cater to the international market. Tower C commenced its first phase of operations in October 2016 with the new Cancer and Radiosurgery Centre and Nuclear Medicine Centre. It also houses other Centres of Excellence such as Digestive Health Centre, Bone and Joint Centre and the expanded Haemodialysis Centre. The second phase, with a 10-level tower, that houses all single-room wards and consultation suites, started its operations in 2017. With the opening of Tower D, E and F in 2023, SMC is Malaysia’s largest private hospital with 724 licensed beds, 492 consultation suites and 26 operating theatres. Tower D contains the country’s first dedicated Children’s Emergency Department in a private hospital as well as a Women and Children’s health block, alongside luxury senior living residences annexed to the hospital. This sets Sunway Medical Centre as one of the leading private medical centres in the region.

Sunway Medical Centre has also won the Orthopaedic Service Provider of the Year Award at the GHT-Bernstein 2017 APAC Healthcare & Medical Tourism Awards, which recognises healthcare providers that have maintained consistently high standards in delivering customer value and demonstrated outstanding performances in initiatives that improve the way healthcare is delivered.

Discover Sunway Medical Centre Velocity

Sunway Medical Centre Velocity is a comprehensive tertiary-care hospital in the township of Sunway Velocity, Cheras. Strategically located in an integrated commercial and residential development nearby Sunway Velocity Mall and Sunway Velocity Hotel, the hospital is less than 4km away from Kuala Lumpur City Centre.

We offer comprehensive tertiary healthcare services with designed capacity of 227 licensed beds, 77 consultant suites and six operating theatres. We also offer advanced medical technologies and state-of-the-art facilities for outpatient and inpatient speciality care, health and wellness programmes, not forgetting our 24-hour emergency services.

We provide patients with the best care, from the initial consultation and diagnosis, through to surgery or treatment and then follow-up care and rehabilitation. Our team of highly experienced healthcare professionals will ensure that your journey here is well coordinated.

We are highly involved in CSR, initiating health programmes consisting of CPR-AED workshops and behavioural health talks. We are also the first hospital in Malaysia to participate in the Race to Zero Campaign, a United Nations (UN) backed initiative aimed at mobilising healthcare institutions around the world to play a leadership role in addressing climate change. These programmes, in line with the UN-SDG, are tailored for the public to educate them on the importance of healthcare – both from a physical and mental aspect.

Part of an Integrated Township

Discover Sunway Home Healthcare

Sunway Home Healthcare is a unique hospital-based home-care structure, and an organised care-at-home facility affiliated to the group of hospitals and centres of Sunway Healthcare Group.

We were established in 2003 with a lean structure of a pure nursing care provider to cater to the unique sphere of services for patients at home. As the need of home healthcare increases due to various factors such as cost, time, convenience, benefits and outcome, we grew to become a multi-disciplinary care provider.

We provide standard wound care, physiotherapy, medical nutrition therapy, or medical care, and even multi-disciplinary services such as post-natal care, home neonatal jaundice phototherapy, vaccination services, short- and long-term on-site nurse services. Additionally, we conduct community public awareness campaigns, rehabilitation treatment, and palliative care delivered by skilled and experienced healthcare professionals.

Our certified, experienced and trustworthy personnel provide a wide range of in-home health services to give you peace of mind as we keep your loved ones safe while living at home.

Discover Sunway Sanctuary

Sunway Healthcare Group (Sunway Healthcare) has rolled out its flagship senior living residence, Sunway Sanctuary, in the second quarter of 2023. Intended as part of our group’s plans to provide for the increasing demand for an exclusive and opulent senior living community in the Klang Valley; and will be part of the greater Sunway City Kuala Lumpur. The facility comprises 473 units with 24/7 concierge services, delectable dining, professional housekeeping and laundry services, maintenance services, IT support, round-the-clock security as well as one-on-one personalised services for various activities and tasks. As part of the focus on holistic wellness, the facility will organise in-house programmes in which each resident will have access to a personal wellness coordinator for a more targeted and customised approach to lifestyle enrichment.

Apart from that, the project will house a wellness centre with healthcare personnel to provide comprehensive clinical assistance including Traditional Complementary Medicine services. For emergency cases, the residents will be referred to Sunway Medical Centre which is annexed to Sunway Sanctuary.

As one of Malaysia’s leading private healthcare providers with more than two decades of experience and expertise in the health and wellness sector, we have an extremely robust infrastructure and resources we can leverage to provide excellent healthcare support.
