TAN SRI DATO’ SERI SIR DR. JEFFREY CHEAH KBE AO

Founder and Chairman, Sunway Group

Tan Sri Sir Dr. Jeffrey Cheah is the visionary founder and chairman of Sunway Group, one of Southeast Asia’s leading conglomerates with core interests in property, construction, education, healthcare, hospitality, and leisure. He is also the founding trustee of Malaysia’s largest education-focused social enterprise, the Jeffrey Cheah Foundation (JCF). His lifelong dedication to sustainable development is evident in the host of thriving communities throughout Sunway’s integrated developments and townships totaling over 2,200 hectares across Malaysia. He has also shown magnanimous philanthropy through JCF, by making quality education accessible to all bright and deserving students for posterity.
