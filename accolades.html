Sunway is the top conglomerate winner at the inaugural The Star ESG Positive Impact Awards 2023, sweeping five awards, including two Gold Awards in the Responsible Consumption & Production and Transformation categories, as well as three Silver Awards in the Water Management & Efficiency; Diversity, Equity & Inclusion and Supply Chain Management categories.

Sunway wins four awards at the National Annual Corporate Report Awards 2023, which promotes excellence and improvement in corporate reporting. Sunway Berhad wins Gold for Best Sustainability Reporting, and Companies with RM2 Billion to RM10 Billion in Market Capitalisation, while Sunway REIT wins Silver. Meanwhile, Sunway Construction Group wins Platinum for the Companies with Less than RM2 Billion in Market Capitalisation.

Sunway Berhad wins Silver in the Industrial Products and Services category, while Sunway REIT wins Silver in the REIT category at The Edge ESG Awards 2023, which recognises Malaysia’s ESG leaders among public-listed companies and asset management firms.

Sunway Construction Group is recognised for Highest Return on Equity over Three Years for the fifth consecutive year at The Edge Billion Ringgit Club 2023, a testament to its management excellence and stellar business performance.

Sunway Berhad wins Silver at the 2023 ARC Awards for the PDF Version of Annual Report (Sustainability Report: Asia/Pacific), demonstrating the highest standards of corporate reporting as benchmarked against global best practices

Sunway wins Conglomerate of the Year in the category of Community Well-being Leadership at the Sustainability & CSR Malaysia Awards 2023 for its community outreach programmes, making it a six-time winner of this coveted award, endorsed by the Ministry of Women, Family and Community Development.

Sunway is named among Malaysia’s Best Managed Companies 2023 by Deloitte Private for the third consecutive year – making it the sole conglomerate to achieve this feat.

Sunway wins Gold under the Sustainability Reporting category for the second year running, and Bronze under the General Award category, at the Australasian Reporting Awards (ARA) 2023, which recognises corporations’ commitment to transparent and sustainable value creation through strategic reporting practices.

Sunway Group clinches five awards at the Putra Brand Awards 2022, including the Putra Brand of the Year Award and Putra Brand Personality of the Year. Meanwhile Sunway Education Group won the Platinum award in the Education & Learning category, with Sunway Property and Sunway Lagoon each taking home the Bronze award in the Property and Entertainment categories respectively.
