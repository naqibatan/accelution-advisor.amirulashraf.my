Why Sunway?

Sunway Group is one of Southeast Asia’s leading conglomerates, with 13 business divisions operating across 50 locations worldwide, primarily across the Asian region.

At Sunway, our passion revolves around positively impacting the lives of our people through growth and development.

We aspire to inspire passion in our people so that they can flourish and prosper in extraordinary ways.

Our core values are what define us and are the reason for our continued success. At Sunway, we urge our people to make their way through life and their careers, with “Integrity” as their guide, “Humility” as their virtue and the pursuit of “Excellence” as their choice.

At Sunway, we actively promote the concept of lifelong learning. We continue to nurture our talents and continue to grow alongside one another with integrity as our guide, humility as our virtue and the pursuit of excellence as our choice.

Tan Sri Dato’ Seri Sir Dr. Jeffrey Cheah KBE AO

Founder and Chairman of Sunway Group

THE SUNWAY STORY

The story of how we begin from a mining wasteland in 1974 to a leading conglomerate in Southeast Asia with our core interests in real estate, construction, education, healthcare, retail, and hospitality. We are committed to advancing the 17 United Nations Sustainable Development Goals and continue to align them with our Environmental, Social, and Governance (ESG) targets as part of our corporate strategy and social responsibility towards driving the group’s long-term success and deepening our commitment to nation-building.

2024 New Year’s Message from Sunway Group Founder and Chairman

This year, Sunway enters a rare category of global companies that have succeeded in marking their 50th anniversary. In his New Year’s message, Sunway Group founder and chairman Tan Sri Sir Dr. Jeffrey Cheah extends his deepest appreciation to the entire Sunway family for their dedication, hard work and passion in making Sunway one of the leading conglomerates in the region. Given Sunway’s track record and the health of its businesses today, Tan Sri Sir Dr. Jeffrey Cheah also expresses his full confidence that the future looks bright for the brand, and that Sunway’s commitment to the sustainability agenda will remain a constant in the decades ahead. Happy New Year!
