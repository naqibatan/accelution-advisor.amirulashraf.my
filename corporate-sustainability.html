Our Commitment to ESG and Sustainability

Sunway has prioritised sustainability since its inception in 1974. It stems from the vision of our founder and chairman, Tan Sri Sir Dr. Jeffrey Cheah, who made sustainability an integral part of Sunway’s business and decision-making process.

We define sustainability as more than just “going green.” Sunway adopts a holistic and comprehensive approach to ensure sustainable long-term growth by leveraging innovation and technology to generate lasting value. And we do so while upholding good governance, environment stewardship, talent management and community investment.

It all begins in sustainable development

Sunway’s commitment to sustainability and core values adds up to a powerful combination that has fuelled the Group’s growth and expansion over half a century. The very founding of Sunway Group is based on the concept of sustainable development, beginning with the transformation of our flagship Sunway City Kuala Lumpur from a wasteland into a wonderland.

We rehabilitated a land scarred by disused and abandoned mining pools, restored the ecosystem both physically and socially, and deployed technology and innovation to build an integrated township that houses everything from educational institutions, commercial entities, medical centre to shopping mall, hotel, themed park and residential neighbourhood. This vibrant community serves more than 200,000 people.

Half Century of Transforming Lives

Our initiative in addressing food security marches on with the Sunway Meal Pack-a-Thon 2022! Alongside Rise Against Hunger Malaysia, the aim is to pack a total of 270,000 meals in 2022 with the help of more than 650 Sunway volunteers across five different locations throughout Malaysia – in line with our pledge to pack two million meals for the underserved communities nationwide.

Underscoring our commitment to the United Nations Sustainable Development Goals, particularly Goal 2: Zero Hunger by 2030, we continue to positively impact millions of beneficiaries across the country in the lead up to our 50th anniversary in 2024.

Bringing HOPE to marginalised Malaysians

In line with the United Nations Sustainable Development Goal 3 of ensuring healthy lives and promoting wellbeing for all, Sunway has committed to a long-term partnership with the Malaysian AIDS Foundation to establish the MAF-Sunway Borneo Health Outreach Programme for Everyone (HOPE) Project. The project addresses the socio-economic barriers to HIV treatment, prevention and care services across the Borneo states of Sabah and Sarawak, and supports the efforts of AIDS eradication throughout Malaysia by 2030.

Our Policies

Sunway Group is committed to upholding the highest standards of integrity, business conduct, and professionalism in line with our core values of Integrity, Humility, and Excellence. We espouse a zero-tolerance policy towards bribery and corruption in our business dealings with all stakeholders governed by the Sunway Anti-Bribery and Corruption (ABC) policy.

Discover more Sunway policies
Anti-Bribery & Corruption Policy

Sunway’s Anti-Bribery & Corruption (ABC) policy was approved by the Board in 2019. It covers the Group’s anti-bribery and corruption strategies as well as action plans in preventing, detecting and managing bribery and corruption issues. Sunway does not make or offer monetary or in-kind political contributions to political parties, political party officials or candidates for political office. In 2020, we rolled out training on the ABC policy to all Sunway staff.

Sustainability Awards

Our commitment and dedicated efforts in promoting sustainability has won us industry recognition. These are important testaments as we aspire to become Asia’s model corporation in sustainable development.

The Star ESG Positive Impact Awards 2023

Sunway is the top conglomerate winner at the inaugural The Star ESG Positive Impact Awards 2023, sweeping five awards, including two Gold Awards in the Responsible Consumption & Production and Transformation categories, as well as three Silver Awards in the Water Management & Efficiency; Diversity, Equity & Inclusion and Supply Chain Management categories.

NACRA Awards 2023

Sunway wins four awards at the National Annual Corporate Report Awards 2023, which promotes excellence and improvement in corporate reporting. Sunway Berhad wins Gold for Best Sustainability Reporting, and Companies with RM2 Billion to RM10 Billion in Market Capitalisation, while Sunway REIT wins Silver. Meanwhile, Sunway Construction Group wins Platinum for the Companies with Less than RM2 Billion in Market Capitalisation.

The Edge ESG Awards 2023

Sunway Berhad wins Silver in the Industrial Products and Services category, while Sunway REIT wins Silver in the REIT category at The Edge ESG Awards 2023, which recognises Malaysia’s ESG leaders among public-listed companies and asset management firms.
